nvNotes

Evolution of an App

nvNotes was launched in 2012 with the initial idea of creating a Note-Taking App that could surpass the default Apple designed Notes App.

Inspired by Notational Velocity; a popular Note-Taking App on the mac. I set out to bring desktop quality software to the mobile platform.

Designed and developed solely by myself, nvNotes was my first App to achieve commercial success and has helped to further my skills as the App itself was updated and enhanced.

Since launch nvNotes has been featured worldwide in news and review sites including Product Hunt, Macstories and more.

In 2017 the Dropbox Sync API was deprecated which sadly meant that nvNotes was no longer viable for further development.
